# License Suspensions

Licenses may be suspended if terms are violated or repeated activation issues occur.

---

## Common causes

- License used on unauthorized domains
- Repeated failed activations
- Billing or provider issues

---

## How to resolve

1. Verify the license key in `.env.local`.
2. Confirm the deployment domain matches the licensed domain.
3. Contact support with project and license details.

Support: **support@zdev.dev**
